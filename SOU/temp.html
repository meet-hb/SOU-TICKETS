General Admission
  • Pricing: Tickets are $10.00 online and $20.00 at the door.
  • Entry Time: Venue entry begins 30 minutes before Garba starts.
  • No Refunds or Exchanges: All ticket sales are final; no refunds or exchanges will be provided.
  • QR Code: Each attendee will receive a unique QR code for entry. Losing your QR code will be considered as not attending the FYMNY Navratri Garba Utsav.
  • Single Entry: Tickets are valid for a single entry only.
  • Free Admission: Children aged 10 years or under enter free of charge.
  • Same-Day Use Only: Tickets are only valid for the date of the event.
  • Hall (Temple) Rules: No smoking or alcohol is allowed inside the hall (temple) .
  • Personal Belongings: Loss of any personal belongings is not the responsibility of the management.
  • Wristband Requirement: Entry without a wristband is not permitted.
  • Re-Entry Policy: To re-enter, you must have a hand stamp.
  • Prohibited Items: Carrying of liquids, alcohol, cigarettes, matchboxes, lighters, e-cigarettes, vapes, and banned substances including food will not be permitted. Dangerous or potentially hazardous objects, including but not limited to weapons, knives, guns, fireworks, helmets, and laser devices, will not be allowed in the venue and may result in ejection with or without the owner.

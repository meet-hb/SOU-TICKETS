Jungle Safari

Location: Statue of Unity Rd, Gujarat 393155, India
Operational Hours: 08:00Hrs to 18:00Hrs
Map
Description

Welcome to the Sardar Patel Zoological Park, a sanctuary of natural wonder and wildlife discovery! Our doors are open from 8 AM to 6 PM, with last entry permitted at 5 PM. Step into a world of immersive, walk-in habitats featuring a vibrant collection of exotic and native birds, as well as primates. Marvel at the unique reptile enclosures in our state-of-the-art Serpentarium. Embark on a captivating journey as you come face-to-face with majestic big cats, and a diverse range of Indian deer & antelopes and African animals such as giraffe, zebra, wildebeest and gemsbok. Don't miss the opportunity to see awe-inspiring mega herbivores like the endangered Indian rhino, Gaur, and African hippo. Explore the intriguing world of incredibly intelligent omnivores, including the Sloth bear, Indian wild dog, and Indian wolf. For your convenience, complimentary e-cart service is available, strictly subject to availability. However, we highly recommend exploring the zoo on foot for a more engaging and enriching experience. Come, join us for an unforgettable day of wildlife adventure!
